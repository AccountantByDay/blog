Accounting Blogs

January 01, 0001

Your directory for accounting blogs! If you don’t see your accounting blog on this page, and would like it to be added to the list, please email me at admin@accountantbyday.com.

Confessions of a CPA / The Tax CPA

Confessions of a CPA is the personal blog of Monica Lawver, tax manager at GALLINA LLP. The blog touches on life as a CPA, but the topics apply to many different careers. The Tax CPA is Monica’s tax blog, which discusses current tax topics.

The Summa

The Summa is written by an accounting professor, David Albrecht. Click your way on through to read about his thoughts on financial accounting regulation, and accounting jokes.

Professor Elam

Professor Elam is an accounting professor who blogs about all different business topics. He posts thoughts and links that are intended to be useful for his accounting students, so if you’re an accounting student, these could be valuable for you too!

Re: The Auditors

This blog is what I think of as the original accounting blog. Very well-connected in the accounting blog-o-sphere, Francine McKenna touches on all kinds of stuff related to accounting, most importantly, current events and what they mean to us accountants.

Tax Girl

A blog about… well, taxes. Kelly Phillips Erb writes about tax issues in understandable language. Also, she finds teachable tax moments in all kinds of odd places.

The Student CPA

A blog written by Narcisse aimed at providing some additional tools, information, and practice aids to accounting students.

Clockwork Accounting

A blog that covers topics relevant to accounting students, or anyone interested in learning more about accounting software or becoming an accountant.

Accounting Diaries

A resource for tutorials on financial accounting, as well as other topics relevant to accountants.
